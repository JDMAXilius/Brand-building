## 01 — IDENTITY STRATEGY STATEMENT

Brand A's identity is elite, precise, and quietly certain — the visual equivalent of the senior engineer who has shipped the hard thing. The organizing principle is confidence through reduction: generous dark space, one disciplined accent, sharp type. Nothing decorative, nothing arbitrary; the restraint is the premium signal, and the craft of the execution is itself a sample of the work Brand A sells.

---

## 02 — LOGO DIRECTION

**Primary direction:** Wordmark-led, high-contrast serif, with a derived monogram for compact use. A single **gold full-stop** device as the signature ("the standard, full stop").

**Character:** Elevated, exact, trustworthy. Engineered proportions; one moment of distinction (the gold dot) that signals craft.

**Style notes:** Constructed and optically corrected, not hand-drawn; tight-but-breathing kerning. High-contrast serif against the sea of geometric-sans tech logos. The gold dot is the only color in the mark.

**What to avoid:** Generic geometric-sans tech wordmarks; literal AI motifs (neural nodes, circuit traces, hexagons, brain/chip, gradients-on-a-cube); isometric 3D; anything that looks generated.

**3 Logo References (borrow one quality from each):**
- **The Economist** — the authoritative high-contrast serif wordmark of a serious, premium institution.
- **Palantir** — restraint and gravity; a quiet mark trusted by enterprise/defense without decoration.
- **Vogue / Dior (serif discipline only)** — the elegance of a high-contrast serif, stripped back toward engineering rather than fashion.

## 03 — COLOR PALETTE *(inherits the Elyoun system)*

**Primary / ground**
- **Obsidian** `#0A0A0C` — near-black with a faint cool cast; premium, serious, infinite.

**Accent**
- **Elyoun Gold** `#C8A96A` (core) / `#E3C98C` (light, for text/gradients on dark) — warm metallic = "the highest standard," craft, value. Used sparingly: logo dot, key lines, highlights — **≤10% of any composition.**

**Neutrals**
- Surface `#15151B` / `#1B1B22` · Hairline `#2A2A31` · Bone text `#ECE8DF` · Muted `#9C988C` · Slate `#6B7A8F` (data/charts only, so gold always means "us/emphasis").

**Light-mode inverse (docs/proposals):** Bone ground `#F7F5F0`, Obsidian text, deepened Gold `#A8884B`.

**Usage rules:** Gold is a spice, not a base. Never gold-on-gold or gold-on-slate. No pure black/white. Maintain WCAG AA (use Light Gold `#E3C98C` for body-size text on dark).

**Palette type:** Monochromatic-neutral + single metallic accent.

---

## 04 — TYPOGRAPHY

- **Display / brand:** **Playfair Display** (high-contrast serif) — hero statements, pull-quotes, big numbers.
- **Headings / UI / labels:** **Space Grotesk** (precise geometric sans) — section labels, kickers (letter-spaced uppercase), buttons, table headers.
- **Body:** **Inter** — paragraphs, documentation, captions, UI.
- **Code / technical:** **JetBrains Mono** (optional) — product/engineering contexts.

**Type personality:** elevated *and* engineered — serif says premium institution, geometric sans says precision instrument, body says clear and credible.

**Avoid:** rounded/friendly sans, system-font brand contexts, slab serifs, more than one accent typeface. Never set body in Playfair.

---

## 05 — IMAGERY & PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE

**Aesthetic:** Cinematic, low-key, precise. Deep shadows, controlled single-source (gold) light, focus and craft.

**Subject matter:** the work/artifacts (code on dark screens, interfaces lit in the dark), people in real concentration, abstract gold-lit craft as texture behind type.

**Color treatment:** dark, desaturated base with warm gold highlights; crushed-but-detailed blacks, no bright whites.

**Avoid:** bright corporate stock, blue-tech clichés (glowing circuits, binary rain, holographic UIs, robot hands), HDR over-production, rainbow gradients.

---

## 06 — ICONOGRAPHY & DESIGN PRINCIPLES

**Iconography:** thin, sharp-cornered line icons on a consistent grid; gold reserved for active/emphasis state; default stroke in Bone/Muted. Prefer diagrams (positioning maps, architecture) over illustration.

**Design principles:**
- **Confidence through reduction** — black space is the luxury; remove, don't add.
- **The accent is earned** — gold marks only what matters most.
- **Precision in every detail** — optical alignment, consistent hairlines, no orphaned type.
- **Elevated, not decorative** — elegance from type, contrast, and proportion, never ornament.

---

## 07 — BRAND EXPRESSIONS

- **Website** — dark-first; obsidian ground, bone text, sparse gold CTAs; Playfair hero + Space Grotesk labels + Inter body; cinematic dark imagery, thin-line diagrams.
- **Stationery** — gold-foil wordmark/dot on heavy black uncoated stock; minimal.
- **Decks** — obsidian slides, ≤10% gold, gold-on-black data viz with slate as the only secondary (see the Elyoun deck).
- **Proposals & technical docs** — light-mode inverse for long-form readability; same type system.
- **Product / UI (Brand A's own software & client systems)** — dark-first UI kit; gold strictly for primary actions and active states.
