## 01 — CORE MESSAGE

*Brand B builds immersive experiences and multiplayer games like engineered systems — so they don't just dazzle, they ship, scale, and stay alive.*

The strategic anchor (not a tagline). Everything below expresses it.

## 02 — VALUE PROPOSITION

Brand B is one studio that combines attraction-grade immersive craft, AAA multiplayer/real-time engineering, and the discipline to deliver — instead of forcing buyers to stitch together a creative studio, a co-dev shop, and a backend specialist who each own half. It treats an experience or a multiplayer game as a system that must perform, scale, and survive real users, and it owns the hard parts end to end.

## 04 — MESSAGING HIERARCHY

**Level 1 — Brand Headline**
> Worlds, built like systems.

**Level 2 — Supporting Statement**
> Brand B engineers immersive experiences and multiplayer games that feel alive and hold up under real users — craft and systems in one studio.

**Level 3 — Key Messages**

**Experiences built like systems.**
An immersive experience is a system: it must perform, scale, and survive opening day. Brand B builds the unseen engineering so the wonder never fails.
*Proof:* Performance/scale benchmarks (fps, concurrency) met in shipped builds. (to confirm)

**Multiplayer we own, end to end.**
Matchmaking, server fleets, live-service, anti-cheat — owned, not subcontracted. The hard backend is a core competency.
*Proof:* Senior multiplayer/netcode bench; server-authoritative architecture in production. (to confirm)

**It ships and it scales.**
A demo that breaks on the floor isn't a deliverable. Brand B ships on time and holds up under real load.
*Proof:* Live builds running daily on the floor / at launch concurrency. (to confirm)

**Craft you can feel, engineering you can trust.**
Fidelity and play on one side, reliability and performance on the other — Brand B refuses to trade either away.
*Proof:* Reel + the metrics behind it.

## 05 — AUDIENCE-SPECIFIC MESSAGING

**Theme parks / attractions**
- **Lead with:** awe + reliability — "flawless on the floor, every single day."
- **Proof that lands:** uptime and performance under real guest load; immersive fidelity.
- **Avoid:** games jargon; treating it as a tech demo.

**Game studios / publishers**
- **Lead with:** senior multiplayer/live-service ownership — "a partner, not a body shop."
- **Proof that lands:** netcode/backend depth, concurrency at launch, owned outcomes.
- **Avoid:** spectacle talk without systems substance.

## 07 — THINGS NOT TO SAY

- **Claims a reel or build can't back** — performance, concurrency, or fidelity we can't demonstrate.
- **Empty superlatives:** "next-level," "AAA-quality" (as a boast), "revolutionary," "magical."
- **"Metaverse" as filler.**
- **Fabricated client lists** — the reel and shipped builds are the proof until names are earned.
- **Spectacle without systems** (to attractions) or **systems without experience** (to studios) — Brand B's whole claim is both.
