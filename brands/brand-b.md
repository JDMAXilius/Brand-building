## PART 1 — STRATEGY

### 1.1 Essence
> *"Worlds and games, built like engineered systems."*

Brand B is a real-time-3D studio that builds **immersive experiences, simulations, and games —
including multiplayer** — with the engineering rigor most experiential studios lack. It spans
attraction/theme-park-grade immersive craft, AAA-grade game development with
**multiplayer/netcode/live-service** engineering, and the AI/software discipline that makes any of
it ship and stay up. The premise: an immersive experience or a multiplayer game is a *system* — it
must perform, scale, and survive real users on opening day — so Brand B builds it like one.

### 1.2 Vision
To be the studio called when an experience has to be both unforgettable *and* unbreakable —
immersive craft held to real engineering standards.

### 1.3 Mission
Design and engineer real-time-3D worlds, simulations, and multiplayer games that ship on time,
scale under load, and feel alive — for studios and operators who can't accept a tech demo.

### 1.4 Values
- **Craft you can feel** — fidelity, performance, and play are the product.
- **Built like systems** — real-time performance, netcode, reliability, not just visuals.
- **Multiplayer-grade engineering** — matchmaking, server fleets, live-service, anti-cheat — owned.
- **Partnership** — embed with the client's team; own the hard parts.
- **Ship it** — an experience that breaks on the floor isn't an experience.

### 1.5 Goals
- Own the position of "immersive craft *and* systems engineering in one studio."
- Land a flagship attraction/experience and a flagship multiplayer/game co-dev as reference reels.
- Build a senior multiplayer/real-time-3D bench that rivals the specialists.
- (Phase 2) launch a productized plugin/tooling line.

## PART 2 — AUDIENCE

### 2.1 Who
Theme-park / attraction Experience & Creative Directors and location-based-entertainment operators;
game-studio CTOs / Technical Directors and publishers. Buyers who need spectacle that also runs.

### 2.2 Personas
**A Creative / Experience Director (attractions).** *"It has to feel real and run flawlessly every
single day on the floor."* Wants awe **and** reliability; burned by demos that don't survive
production.

**A game-studio CTO / Technical Director.** *"I need senior engineers who can own multiplayer and
live-service — a partner, not a body shop."* Wants AAA backend/netcode depth and co-dev that
behaves like a stakeholder.

### 2.3 What they care about
Realism and emotional impact; performance under real load; multiplayer that holds up; a partner who
owns the hard systems rather than handing back fragile work.

## PART 3 — POSITIONING

### 3.1 Statement
*Brand B is the real-time-3D studio that builds immersive experiences and multiplayer games like
engineered systems — one partner combining attraction-grade craft, AAA multiplayer engineering, and
the rigor to make it ship and scale.*

### 3.2 Map
- **X:** pure creative/experiential craft ←→ deep software/multiplayer engineering
- **Y:** execution arm ←→ strategic build partner
- Brand B sits **top-center**: deliberately strong on *both* axes — craft **and** systems
  engineering — where rivals pick one.

### 3.3 Competitors *(see `../COMPETITIVE_LANDSCAPE.md` §1–2)*
Enludio (immersive/RT-3D craft, light on AI/software rigor & security); Side/PTW (scale game
services, execution-led); Companion Group (senior multiplayer/backend — the premium specialist to
beat on engineering); Devoted/Pingle (co-dev capacity); Theory/Talon (Orlando immersive).

### 3.4 How Brand B stands out
Immersive studios bring spectacle but break under load and lack AI/security rigor; game outsourcers
bring capacity but not partnership or strategy; multiplayer specialists own the backend but not the
experience. Brand B is built for the fusion buyers increasingly need — AI inside immersive,
games-quality realism inside training, consumer polish on systems that must not fail — held to one
standard: it ships, it scales, it's alive.

## PART 4 — PERSONALITY & VOICE

### 4.1 Personality
**Axis:** Imaginative · Bold · Technically deep · Confident. More expressive and energetic than
Brand A — this audience lives in creativity — but still craft-led and substance-first, never hypey.
The maker who can both *dream the world* and *make it run*.

### 4.2 Voice essence
*Brand B sounds like a senior creative-technologist who has shipped worlds — vivid about the
experience, exact about the engineering, allergic to empty hype.*

### 4.3 Voice qualities (with do / don't)
- **Vivid** — *Do:* "A crowd of 200 moving through one persistent world, 60 fps, no seams." *Don't:*
  "An amazing, immersive, next-level experience."
- **Exact under the magic** — name the engine, the frame budget, the player count, the latency.
- **Confident, not loud** — let the reel and the build carry it; state, don't oversell.
- **Player/guest-first** — center what the audience *feels and does*, not the toolchain.
- **Honest about the hard parts** — netcode, scale, and live-ops are where projects die; say so.

### 4.4 Tone dimensions
Formality: mid — polished but human, creative-industry-native. Energy: noticeably more energetic
than Brand A; momentum without exclamation-mark hype. Expertise: deep (engine, netcode, rendering)
flexing to evocative for creative buyers. Warmth: collaborative, "we'll build it with you." Humor:
allowed, smart, never at the work's expense.

### 4.5 Vocabulary
**Use:** world · real-time · immersive · multiplayer · netcode · live-service · frame budget ·
fidelity · persistent · scale · ship · build · play · engine (Unreal/Unity) · performance ·
on the floor. **Avoid:** "next-level," "AAA-quality" (as an empty boast), "revolutionary,"
"metaverse" (as filler), "seamless," "magical" (overused), and any claim a reel can't back.

### 4.6 Style rules
Lead with the experience, follow with the proof. Specifics over adjectives (numbers, engines,
player counts). Short punchy lines welcome; em-dashes fine. Exclamation marks: rare and earned.
Active voice. Show, don't tell — pair claims with footage/metrics.

### 4.7 Before / after
- *Off:* "Our revolutionary metaverse platform delivers next-level immersive experiences." → *On:*
  "A persistent world for 200 concurrent guests at 60 fps — built in Unreal, live on the floor."

## PART 6 — VISUAL IDENTITY *(distinct from Brand A — NOT black + gold)*

### 6.1 Identity statement
Cinematic, vivid, and engineered. Where Brand A is restrained and premium, Brand B is *alive* —
depth, light, and motion — but every effect is intentional, the way a great render is. Spectacle
with craft, never noise.

### 6.2 Logo
A confident, contemporary mark with a sense of depth or dimension — a wordmark with a structural/
spatial device (e.g., parallax layering, an isometric or volumetric cue) rather than a flat tech
logotype. Should look as good on a game splash screen as on an attraction wall.

### 6.3 Color
- **Base:** deep cinematic dark — Void `#0B0D14` (deep indigo-black), not the warm obsidian of A.
- **Signature accent:** a vivid **spectral/iridescent** range — electric cyan `#2FE6E0` →
  ultraviolet `#7B5Cff` → magenta `#FF3DAE`, used as light/energy (gradients, glows, edge light).
- Neutrals: cool grays `#161922` / `#222736`, off-white `#EAF0FF`.
- Rule: the spectrum reads as *real-time light*, not rainbow gradients — directional, motivated,
  used to create depth. More color than A allows, still disciplined.

### 6.4 Typography
- **Display:** a characterful contemporary sans or techno-display (e.g., a wide geometric or a
  precise techno face) — more expressive than Playfair, with presence on a title card.
- **Body/UI:** a clean neutral sans (Inter or similar).
- **Mono:** for build specs / technical credibility.

### 6.5 Imagery & motion
Real-time-3D worlds, in-engine captures, volumetric light, depth-of-field, motion. Show *the world
and the player/guest in it.* Motion is a first-class brand asset — loops, parallax, real-time
captures. Avoid static stock and flat illustration.

### 6.6 Iconography & principles
Dimensional, slightly more playful than A's, but consistent and grid-based. Principles: depth over
flatness · light with intent · show the world · spectacle earned by craft · motion is brand.

### 6.7 Expressions
Reel-first website with live captures; bold expo/booth presence; splash/title treatments; social
clips of builds; a UI kit for any owned tools/plugins that feels like a pro creative tool.
