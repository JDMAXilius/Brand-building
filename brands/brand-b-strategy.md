# Brand B — Brand Strategy
### Real-time 3D, Immersive & Games *(name TBD)*

> One of three independent companies under the silent Elyoun Group (see `BRAND_ARCHITECTURE.md`).
> Gets its OWN brand system — likely more expressive than Brand A (a creative/games audience
> rewards distinctiveness over premium restraint). 2026-06-28.

---

## 01 — WHAT IS BRAND B?
> *"Worlds and games, built like engineered systems."*

Brand B is a real-time-3D studio that builds **immersive experiences, simulations, and games —
including multiplayer** — with the engineering rigor most experiential studios lack. It spans three
things that usually live in separate vendors: attraction- and theme-park-grade immersive craft,
AAA-grade game development and **multiplayer/netcode/live-service** engineering, and the AI and
software discipline that makes any of it ship and stay up. The premise: an immersive experience or
a multiplayer game is a *system* — it has to perform, scale, and survive real users on opening day —
so Brand B builds it like one.

---

## 02 — VISION
*To be the studio called when an experience has to be both unforgettable and unbreakable —
immersive craft held to real engineering standards.*

## 03 — MISSION
*Design and engineer real-time-3D worlds, simulations, and multiplayer games that ship on time,
scale under load, and feel alive — for the studios and operators who can't accept a tech demo.*

## 04 — VALUES
- **Craft you can feel** — fidelity, performance, and play are the product.
- **Built like systems** — real-time performance, netcode, and reliability, not just visuals.
- **Multiplayer-grade engineering** — matchmaking, server fleets, live-service, anti-cheat — owned,
  not outsourced.
- **Partnership** — embed with the client's team; own the hard parts.
- **Ship it** — an experience that breaks on the floor isn't an experience.

## 05 — TARGET AUDIENCE
**Primary buyers:** theme-park / attraction Experience & Creative Directors and LBE operators;
game-studio CTOs / Technical Directors and publishers.

**Persona — a Creative/Experience Director (attractions).** *"It has to feel real and run flawlessly
every single day on the floor."* Wants spectacle **and** reliability.

**Persona — a game-studio CTO.** *"I need senior engineers who can own multiplayer and live-service,
not a body shop."* Wants AAA backend/netcode depth and co-dev that behaves like a partner.

## 06 — PERSONALITY
**Axis:** Imaginative · Bold · Technically deep · Confident. More expressive and energetic than
Brand A — this audience lives in creativity — but still craft-led and substance-first, never hypey.

## 07 — VOICE *(own voice — do not inherit Elyoun's verbatim)*
Vivid and concrete; speaks about worlds, play, and performance with the confidence of having
shipped them. Shows the work (reels, builds, metrics) over adjectives. Energetic where Brand A is
calm — but the same allergy to buzzwords and the same proof-over-hype discipline.

## 08 — COMPETITORS *(see `COMPETITIVE_LANDSCAPE.md` §1–2)*
Enludio (immersive/RT-3D craft, light on AI/software rigor & security); Side/PTW (scale game
services, execution-led); Companion Group (senior multiplayer/backend — the premium specialist to
beat on engineering); Devoted Studios / Pingle (co-dev capacity); Theory/Talon (Orlando immersive).

## 09 — POSITIONING
**Statement:** *Brand B is the real-time-3D studio that builds immersive experiences and
multiplayer games like engineered systems — one partner combining attraction-grade craft, AAA
multiplayer engineering, and the rigor to make it ship and scale.*

**Map:** X = pure creative/experiential craft ←→ deep software/multiplayer engineering; Y =
execution arm ←→ strategic build partner. Brand B sits top-center: it is the only one deliberately
strong on *both* axes — craft **and** systems engineering — where rivals pick one.

## 10 — HOW BRAND B STANDS OUT
Immersive studios bring spectacle but break under load and lack security/AI rigor; game
outsourcers bring capacity but not partnership or strategy; multiplayer specialists own the
backend but not the experience. Brand B is built for the fusion buyers increasingly need — AI
inside immersive, games-quality realism inside training, consumer polish on systems that must not
fail — and holds all of it to one standard: it ships, it scales, it's alive.

## 11 — TAGLINE DIRECTIONS
- "Worlds, built like systems." *(lead)*
- "Real-time. Real craft."
- "Play, engineered."
- "Immersion that ships."

## 12 — PRODUCT LINE (Phase 2, optional) — digital plugins / tooling
Once the services brand has traction, productize the engine plugins, SDKs, and tools built along
the way (Unreal/Unity) as recurring products — the "ships its own products" flywheel applied to
real-time 3D. Services-first; add a tool only when there's a real one worth shipping.

## 13 — PROOF POSTURE & GRADUATION
Portfolio/showreel-led — the work *is* the pitch. Brand B graduates from an internal practice to a
full, independent company once it has a flagship immersive/games project **and** a named buyer.
Until then it can be incubated inside the group without funding a separate brand engine. No
fabricated client lists; the reel and shipped builds are the proof.
