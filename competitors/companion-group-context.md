## Brand
- **Name**: Companion Group
- **Category**: AAA game co-development — multiplayer/backend/online-services engineering
- **Description**: Employee-owned game-development studio providing AAA co-development, multiplayer engineering, and backend/online services; Unreal networking and live-service specialists who embed with client teams.
- **Stage**: Growth; UK-linked (UK Games Fund "Project Dual")
- **Website**: companiongroup.io
- **Tagline**: "Technical minds [POWERED_BY] Human collaboration" _(verified)_ · sub: "Your companion to building the next generation of successful games." _(verified)_

## Audience
- **Primary Audience**: AAA / live-service game studios and publishers needing senior engineering capacity.
- **Buyer Titles**: CTO, Technical Director, Lead/Principal Engineer, Studio Head.
- **Key Problem**: Hard online/multiplayer/backend engineering studios can't staff or solve in-house.
- **Their Language** _(verified)_: "GBaaS" (game-backend-as-a-service), "bespoke backends," "online services," "multiplayer," "gameplay," "cloud," "integrate," "augment your team," "take full ownership," "companion." Plus the inferred-still-apt: "Unreal networking," "game-server fleets," "live service," "matchmaking." A code/mono motif in copy: `[00]`, `[+]`, `//`.

## Positioning
- **Differentiation**: Senior engineers from Xbox, Amazon, Google, Tencent, EA, Creative Assembly; employee-ownership as a trust/quality signal; embed-and-own model.
- **Positioning Statement**: The senior, employee-owned engineering partner that owns the hardest AAA online/live-service work. _(inferred)_
- **Competitors**: Side, Devoted Studios, Pingle Studio, StudioKrew; and Elyoun (games segment).
- **Market Position**: Premium / specialist (vs. scale-and-capacity rivals).

## Brand Personality
- **Personality Words**: Senior, technical, trustworthy, owner-minded — but **playful and human**, not austere _(verified-refined)_
- **Axis**: Specialist · Deeply-technical · Energetic · Partnership-led
- **Tone**: Engineer-to-engineer precision wrapped in a friendly, gamer-dev brand — a mascot ("companion" robot), "human collaboration," code/mono flourishes. Warmer and more brand-forward than the earlier "anti-marketing/understated" inference. _(verified-refined)_

## Brand Voice

- **Voice Qualities**: Technical · Precise · Owner-minded · Human/playful _(verified-refined)_
