## 00 — RECOMMENDED STRUCTURE FOR THE "ELYOUN" NAME

You're open to renaming all three operating companies — so the cleanest move is:

> **Elyoun becomes the group / holding name** (e.g. *Elyoun Group*), not an operating brand.
> All three customer-facing companies get fresh, focused names.

Why this is the best use of the name:
- It **preserves Elyoun** (you like it) while freeing each operating company to be focused and
  un-grandiose.
- It **resolves the grandiosity critique completely**: "the highest / the standard" is exactly
  right as an *invisible parent ethos* — the standard all three are held to — and wrong as a
  customer-facing product name.
- It matches the house-of-brands model: the holdco is silent to customers, visible only to
  investors, recruits, and partners (where "the highest standard" is an asset, not a boast).

_(If you'd rather keep Elyoun visible as one operating company instead, assign it to Company A and
take that name off A's shortlist below — everything else holds.)_

## 01 — NAMING CRITERIA (ALL COMPANIES)

1. **Focused** — evokes the one domain, not "everything."
2. **Sayable & spellable** — one unambiguous pronunciation; no "Elyoun" ambiguity.
3. **Distinctive & ownable** — not a crowded word in that exact space (checked below).
4. **Un-grandiose** — confidence through the work, not the name (matches the voice).
5. **Domain/TM-plausible** — real path to a .com or strong variant + a clean trademark class.
